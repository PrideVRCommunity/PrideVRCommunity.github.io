Creating Inclusive and Empowering Spaces through VR PrideVR - A LGBTQ+ VR organization

Welcome to PrideVR, where we're dedicated to cultivating an inclusive LGBTQ+ virtual reality (VR) community. Through the immersive platform of VRChat, we offer a growing range of events and initiatives that connect individuals, celebrate diversity, and empower LGBTQ+ voices. Our goal is to create a vibrant and supportive virtual space that transcends physical limitations, allowing people from around the world to unite in celebration.

As a new organization, we're working diligently to curate an exciting lineup of activities within VRChat. Our events include virtual Pride parades, engaging panel discussions, workshops, and social gatherings. We collaborate with talented queer artists and community leaders who share our vision of inclusivity and empowerment.

At PrideVR, we prioritize creating a safe and welcoming environment for individuals of all backgrounds. We strive to provide a platform for self-expression, personal growth, and connection. Join us in shaping a virtual reality space that celebrates love, acceptance, and equality. Whether you're a seasoned VRChat enthusiast or new to the VR world, PrideVR invites you to be part of our evolving community. Together, let's create unforgettable experiences and pave the way for an inclusive future in virtual reality.
