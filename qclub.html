PrideVR's VRC LGBTQ+ Events Group 🌈✨ About QClub

Where mature VR gamers come together for fun, supportive, and inclusive experiences. Join us for our weekly Wednesday night Socials 🎉, Saturday VRMovie Chill 🍿, and Tuesday VRC gaming nights 🎮, as we build a vibrant calendar of LGBTQ+ events, artists, and performers in VR 🗓️🎨🎤. Led by a passionate team, we're dedicated to creating inclusive Queer VR experiences 🌟🤝, and we welcome artists and developers to join us in shaping an exciting future for the LGBTQ+ community in virtual reality. 🌈🔮
